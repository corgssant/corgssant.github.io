## City of Dexter or the Township

A Dexter address can sit inside the city or out in the surrounding townships. Inside the city limits it's the City of Dexter. Outside them, the parcel usually falls under Dexter, Webster, Scio, or Lima Township, and each runs its own building office. We sort out the right one from your parcel and bring the plot plan and application to the job.
