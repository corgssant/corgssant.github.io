## CNO Pole Barns in Oakland Township

CNO Pole Barns serves Oakland Township as part of a broader Oakland County service area that also reaches into Genesee, Lapeer, and Macomb counties. The company was established in 1970 and is now locally owned and operated by Zosim Serban, who runs every job alongside one of CNO's 3 crews. We build pole barns, horse barns, garages, and steel buildings, licensed and insured with an A+ BBB rating behind us.

## Equestrian Estates Along the Paint Creek Corridor

Oakland Township's large-parcel zoning and the Paint Creek corridor have kept a lot of the township in horses, pasture, and open land, and the buildings we get asked about reflect that. Horse barns with multiple stalls, a wash bay, and hay storage are a common request, sized around the number of animals a property keeps. Detached garages, equipment buildings, and workshops round out the rest, usually on the larger parcels that give a property room for more than one outbuilding. We also erect Federal Steel buildings as a certified FSS dealer, which can make sense for an indoor arena or a wide equipment building where a post-frame clear span runs out of room.

## Questions Oakland Township Owners Ask

### How many stalls can CNO fit in a horse barn?
That comes down to your lot and how you want the barn laid out. Stall count, aisle width, and whether you want a wash bay or tack room all get worked out during the free on-site estimate.

### Is a permit required for a barn over 200 square feet?
Yes, Oakland Township requires a building permit once a detached accessory building passes 200 square feet. We handle the application and the inspections that go with it.

### Does CNO only build post-frame barns, or steel too?
Both. CNO is a certified Federal Steel Systems dealer, so if a project calls for a longer clear span than post-frame construction supports, we can quote a steel building instead.

### What if my property backs up to Paint Creek or has wetland on it?
Tell us during the site visit. We'll look at the buildable area on your parcel and design around any setback or drainage issue before we put together a quote.

## Permit Requirements in Oakland Township

Oakland Township requires a building permit for any detached accessory building over 200 square feet, a threshold nearly every pole barn or horse barn clears. We handle the permit application and the inspections that follow, so the township's process doesn't fall on you.
