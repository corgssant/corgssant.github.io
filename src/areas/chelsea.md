## CNO Pole Barns in Chelsea

CNO Pole Barns is based in Clarkston, about an hour from Chelsea, and the farm country of western Washtenaw County is part of our service area. We build horse barns, agricultural pole barns, and shops for property owners around the city and the townships that surround it. The business has been building in Michigan since 1970 and is locally owned today by Zosim Serban, who runs jobs with one of our 3 crews.

## Farm and Horse Country

Chelsea sits in the open western half of Washtenaw County, with the Waterloo Recreation Area to the northwest and working farms and horse properties spread across the townships. That ground shapes the work. A [horse barn](/services/horse-barns/) with stalls and a tack room, an [agricultural pole barn](/services/pole-barns/) for hay and equipment, or a shop for projects and storage. Post-frame handles most of it and takes insulation well when you want the building usable year-round. For a riding arena or a wide clear-span shop, we quote, deliver, and stand up a [Federal Steel Systems building](/steel-buildings/) as a certified dealer.

## City of Chelsea or the Townships

A Chelsea mailing address can sit inside the city or out in the surrounding farm townships. Inside the city limits it's the City of Chelsea. Outside them, the parcel usually falls under Sylvan, Lima, Dexter, or Lyndon Township, and each runs its own building office. We pin down the right one from your parcel and bring the plot plan and application to the job.
