## CNO Pole Barns, Roughly 40 Minutes from Lapeer

CNO Pole Barns is based in Clarkston, roughly 35 to 45 minutes south of the city of Lapeer, and Lapeer County has been part of our regular service area. We build [pole barns](/services/pole-barns/), agricultural buildings, and [detached garages](/services/garages/) for property owners around the county seat, designed around the site rather than pulled from a catalog. The business dates to 1970. Today it's owned and run by Zosim Serban, who works alongside one of our 3 crews on every job.

## Permitting Through the City of Lapeer

The City of Lapeer's Building Department handles accessory structure permits as its own step, separate from planning review, and publishes its own guidance for accessory buildings. If a property sits outside city limits in Lapeer Township or another jurisdiction, the process runs through that township's building department instead. We confirm which jurisdiction a project falls under before we pull anything.

## What Lapeer County Properties Are Building

Lapeer County still runs on working farms, and that shows in what we get asked to build here: equipment barns for tractors and implements, hay and livestock buildings, and pole barns that double as a shop for whatever the operation needs that season. We also build detached garages and storage buildings for the more residential parcels closer to town, but the acreage around Lapeer keeps the agricultural requests steady.
