Post-frame handles all of it and goes up faster than stick framing. For a larger clear-span building, we quote, deliver, and stand up [Federal Steel Systems buildings](/steel-buildings/) as a certified dealer.

## City of Brighton or the Township

A Brighton mailing address doesn't tell you who reviews the permit. Inside the city limits it's the City of Brighton. Outside them, the parcel usually falls under Brighton Township or Genoa Township, and each runs its own building department. We pin down the right office from your parcel before we submit, and the plot plan and application come with the job.
