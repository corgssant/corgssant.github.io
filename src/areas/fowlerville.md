## Village of Fowlerville or the Township

A Fowlerville address can sit inside the village or out in the surrounding township. Inside the village limits it's the Village of Fowlerville. Outside them, the parcel usually falls under Handy Township or a neighboring township, and each reviews its own permits. We sort out the right office from your parcel and bring the plot plan and application to the job.
