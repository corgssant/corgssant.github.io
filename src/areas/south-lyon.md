## Pole Barns for South Lyon and Western Oakland County

South Lyon occupies a unique position in Oakland County — close enough to metro Detroit for an easy commute, but far enough west that properties still have the space and rural feel that make pole barns both practical and popular. CNO Pole Barns, located about 30 minutes north in Clarkston, has been building throughout the South Lyon area for decades. We serve homeowners in the city of South Lyon as well as the surrounding communities of Lyon Township, New Hudson, Milford, and the Green Oak Township border.

## Where Suburban Meets Country

South Lyon's western Oakland County location means we see a wide range of property types. Near downtown and in the newer subdivisions east of Pontiac Trail, lots tend to be smaller and neighborhoods have specific aesthetic standards. Farther out in Lyon Township and along the Milford corridor, properties open up to 5, 10, and 20+ acres with room for serious outbuildings. We tailor our designs to both environments — building compact, attractive garages that complement suburban homes and larger agricultural structures that serve working rural properties.

## Popular Projects in the South Lyon Area

South Lyon homeowners frequently ask us for detached garages with extra bays for storage, workshops with dedicated electrical and lighting, and hobby barns for everything from car restoration to small-scale farming. The area's equestrian community is also active, and we build horse barns with custom stall configurations, tack rooms, and hay lofts. For the area's remaining active farms and larger rural parcels, we construct equipment barns, hay storage buildings, and multi-purpose agricultural structures built to handle heavy daily use.
