## CNO Pole Barns in Hartland

CNO Pole Barns works out of Clarkston, about 35 minutes east of Hartland, and the area is part of our regular Livingston County service territory. We build detached garages, pole barns, and horse barns for property owners on Hartland's larger lots and rural parcels. The business has been building in Michigan since 1970, and owner Zosim Serban runs every job with one of our 3 crews.

## Room to Build on Hartland Acreage

Hartland keeps a lot of open ground between its subdivisions, out where US-23 and M-59 cross, and that acreage is where most of our work goes. A [garage](/services/garages/) with space for a lift, a [pole barn](/services/pole-barns/) for equipment and toys, or a [horse barn](/services/horse-barns/) with stalls and a tack room, built for how you use the property. Post-frame construction goes up efficiently and takes insulation well if you want the building heated. For a wider clear-span shop or arena, we quote, deliver, and stand up a [Federal Steel Systems building](/steel-buildings/) instead.

## Permits Through Hartland Township

Hartland Township handles its own building department, so most permits in the area run through one office. The township reviews zoning and a scaled plot plan before it issues the building permit. We put that paperwork together at the estimate so it doesn't stall the build once we're set to start.
