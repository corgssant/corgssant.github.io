## Permits: City of Grand Blanc vs. Grand Blanc Township

The City of Grand Blanc and Grand Blanc Township are two separate jurisdictions with two separate building departments, even though they share a name and sit next to each other. Before finalizing a Grand Blanc-area estimate, we confirm which one a property actually falls under, since that determines which permit process applies.
