## A short trip from our Clarkston base

Waterford Township borders Independence Township, home of CNO's Clarkston base, so this is about as close to home as our work gets. Zosim Serban owns and runs the company today, working with one of CNO's 3 crews. CNO has been building since 1970 and is licensed, insured, and A+ rated with the Better Business Bureau.

## A township of lakes needs a place for the boats

Waterford has one of the highest lake counts in Oakland County. Cass Lake and Elizabeth Lake are the big names, and dozens of smaller lakes fill in the rest of the township. All that water means boats and campers that sit idle from October through April, and most of the calls we get here are for [boat and RV storage](/services/boat-rv-storage/): a detached garage sized for the boat and trailer, or a taller bay the camper can pull into.

## Tight lake lots, honest answers

Plenty of Waterford parcels are tight lake lots, and a tight lot changes the plan. Setbacks off the water and lot coverage rules decide the footprint before anything else, so the realistic build is often a [detached garage](/services/garages/) or storage building shaped to the lot rather than a big barn. We measure what the parcel allows during the free estimate and design inside it.

## Permits through Waterford Township

Waterford Township handles permits for pole barns, garages, and other accessory buildings, and we take care of the drawings, the application, and the inspections. Jobs across the township line in White Lake, Clarkston, Pontiac, or West Bloomfield go through their own offices, and we sort that out the same way.
