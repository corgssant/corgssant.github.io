## The biggest city in our Genesee County range

Flint sits at the top of CNO's Genesee County territory, and the work here splits cleanly along the city line. Inside Flint itself, lots run small and the common call is a [detached garage](/services/garages/): a new build where one never stood, or a replacement for a garage that's past saving. Out in the townships that ring the city, parcels stretch out and [pole barns](/services/pole-barns/) take over for equipment, workshops, and storage.

## Garage work the city actually needs

A lot of Flint's housing stock went up decades ago, and plenty of its garages did too. We build two-car and larger detached garages sized to the lot, framed for Michigan snow loads, with concrete, electrical, and overhead doors handled in one contract. If the old structure needs to come down first, our demolition crew takes care of that and the rebuild in one job.

## Permits in Flint and the surrounding townships

Addresses inside the city limits go through the City of Flint's building department. Burton, Flint Township, Mundy Township, Genesee Township, and Davison each run their own offices, and the requirements differ enough that it pays to know which desk you're dealing with. We sort that out before anything gets submitted and prepare the drawings and site plan ourselves.

## Already in the neighborhood

Our crews run Genesee County every week: Grand Blanc is just south, Fenton anchors the bottom of the county, and Holly sits across the Oakland line. Flint is inside that same loop, not a stretch.
