## Garages and shops for eastern Oakland County

CNO Pole Barns builds detached garages, workshops, and pole barns across Oakland County, and Rochester Hills sits on the county's eastern side inside that service area. Zosim Serban owns and runs the company today, working with one of CNO's 3 crews. The business has been building since 1970 and is licensed, insured, and A+ rated with the Better Business Bureau.

## What actually fits on a Rochester Hills lot

Most of Rochester Hills is subdivision lots, and we'd rather say that plainly than pretend every backyard can hold a barn. On a typical lot, the project that makes sense is a [detached garage](/services/garages/) or a workshop that fits inside the city's setback and lot coverage rules. Plenty of subdivisions here also carry HOA restrictions on detached buildings, and that's worth checking before you get attached to a plan. We look at all of it during the free estimate and tell you what the lot will take.

Larger parcels do exist. They sit toward the Oakland Township border and along the Clinton River corridor, and those properties can handle a full [pole barn](/services/pole-barns/) or storage building.

## Permits through the City of Rochester Hills

Building permits for detached garages and accessory buildings go through the City of Rochester Hills building department. We handle that end of the job: the site plan, the construction drawings, the application, and the inspections that follow. The city reviews placement against its zoning rules before a permit is issued, which is why we settle where the building sits early. If your property turns out to be over the line in Rochester, Oakland Township, Auburn Hills, or Troy, we sort out the right office before anything gets filed.
