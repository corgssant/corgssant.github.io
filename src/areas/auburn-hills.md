## Where the I-75 corridor meets the neighborhoods

Auburn Hills is two kinds of town at once. Along I-75 and M-59 it's one of Oakland County's busiest business corridors, and off the corridors it's residential streets and the occasional larger parcel toward the Rochester Hills and Orion Township borders. We build for both sides of that split.

## Garages for the residential side

On the residential streets, the call is usually a [detached garage](/services/garages/): a second garage for the toys, a workshop bay, or storage the house can't hold. City zoning sets the limits on size and placement, so we start every Auburn Hills quote by checking what your lot allows and sizing the building to fit inside those lines instead of guessing.

## Post-frame and steel for the business side

For shops, contractors' yards, and storage operations, [commercial post-frame buildings](/services/commercial-buildings/) keep the cost down and go up fast. When a project outgrows wood, we quote, deliver, and stand up pre-engineered [Federal Steel buildings](/steel-buildings/) as a certified FSS dealer. Since we sell both systems, the recommendation follows the project, not our inventory.

## Permits through the city

Building permits in Auburn Hills run through the city's own offices rather than a township hall. We put the package together, from the site plan to truss details, and handle the back-and-forth of review ourselves.
