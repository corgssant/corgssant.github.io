## Michigan's Trusted Pole Barn Builder Since 1970

CNO Pole Barns has been building high-quality pole barns across Southeast Michigan for over 55 years. Founded by Zosim Serban in Clarkston, MI, our family-owned business brings decades of craftsmanship to every project. Whether you need a barn for agricultural storage, a workshop, or a multipurpose building, we design and construct pole barns that stand the test of time.

## Built to Last in Michigan's Climate

Our pole barns are engineered to withstand Michigan's harsh winters, heavy snow loads, and spring storms. We use premium treated posts set deep into the ground, commercial-grade steel roofing, and durable siding to ensure your building performs for decades. Every structure is built to meet or exceed local building codes in Oakland County and the surrounding region.

## A Pole Barn for Every Need

From hobby farms in Clarkston to large rural properties in northern Oakland County, our pole barns serve a wide range of purposes. Use yours as a hay barn, equipment shelter, vehicle storage, or even a future living space. We offer fully customizable floor plans, door placements, window options, and interior finishes so your pole barn works exactly the way you need it to.
