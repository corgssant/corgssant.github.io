## Out with the Old, In with the New

Sometimes the best thing you can do for your property is remove a structure that has outlived its usefulness. CNO Pole Barns provides safe, efficient demolition services for old barns, sheds, garages, and other outbuildings across Oakland County. Whether you are clearing a site for new construction or simply removing a hazard, our crew handles the job from start to finish, including debris removal and site cleanup.

## Safe and Responsible Demolition

Tearing down a building is not as simple as pushing it over with a bulldozer. Structural demolition requires planning, proper equipment, and attention to safety. CNO Pole Barns evaluates each structure before demolition begins, identifying potential hazards like unstable walls, buried utilities, and hazardous materials. We work methodically to bring structures down safely and efficiently, keeping your property and neighboring properties protected throughout the process.

## Seamless Transition to New Construction

Many of our demolition clients are clearing their property to make room for a new pole barn, garage, or shop. Because CNO Pole Barns handles both demolition and new construction, the transition from tear-down to build-up is seamless. We can grade the site, pour new footings, and begin construction as soon as the old structure is gone. This single-contractor approach saves you time, money, and the hassle of coordinating multiple companies. Zosim Serban and the CNO team have been doing this work in the Clarkston area since 1970.
