## Simple, Strong, and Affordable

Not every project calls for a fully enclosed building. CNO Pole Barns builds pole sheds that provide the shelter and storage you need without the cost of a full barn. These open-sided or partially enclosed structures are perfect for protecting hay, firewood, farm equipment, and vehicles from Michigan's rain, snow, and sun. We use the same quality posts, trusses, and metal roofing as our larger buildings, just in a streamlined design.

## Versatile Designs for Farm and Home

Our pole sheds range from simple three-sided run-in shelters for livestock to large open equipment storage buildings. Add a back wall for wind protection. Add sliding doors for security. Keep it completely open for easy drive-through access. The modular nature of post-frame construction means we can build exactly what you need without paying for features you do not want.

## Fast Construction, Lasting Value

Because pole sheds require less material and labor than fully enclosed buildings, they are among the quickest structures we build. Many pole sheds are completed in just a week or two. That speed does not come at the expense of quality. CNO Pole Barns has been building agricultural structures in Oakland County since 1970, and our pole sheds are built to handle decades of Michigan weather.
