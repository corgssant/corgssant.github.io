## Simple, Open Storage Built to Last

Not every project needs a fully enclosed building. CNO Pole Barns builds pole sheds, open-sided or partially enclosed structures that protect hay, equipment, and vehicles from Michigan weather without the cost of a full barn. We use the same posts, trusses, and steel roofing on a pole shed as we do on our larger buildings, just in a smaller, simpler design.

## Open, Partially Enclosed, or Fully Enclosed

You can leave a pole shed fully open for easy drive-through access, add a back wall for wind protection, or close it in further with sliding doors for added security. Because the design is modular, we build to the level of enclosure you actually need instead of a fixed template.
