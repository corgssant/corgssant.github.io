## Metal Roofing You Can Count On

A roof is only as good as the crew that installs it. CNO Pole Barns has been installing metal roofs on pole barns, garages, horse barns, and commercial buildings across Southeast Michigan for over 55 years. We use high-quality steel panels with factory-applied paint finishes that resist fading, chalking, and corrosion. Whether you need a roof on a new construction project or a replacement on an aging building, we deliver a watertight result every time.

## Re-Roofing and Replacement Services

Michigan weather takes a toll on roofs. Heavy snow loads, ice dams, wind-driven rain, and summer storms can all shorten the life of your building's roof. If your pole barn, garage, or agricultural building has a roof that is leaking, sagging, or past its prime, CNO Pole Barns can replace it with a new metal roof system. We handle everything from tear-off and structural repair to final trim and gutter installation.

## The Right Roof for Your Building

We offer multiple panel profiles including standing seam, AG panel, and PBR panel in dozens of colors. Our roofing systems include proper ridge ventilation, drip edge, and snow guards where needed.
