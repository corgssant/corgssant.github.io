## Siding, Trim, and Finish Work

CNO Pole Barns installs and repairs siding, trim, soffit, and fascia for pole barns, garages, horse barns, and homes across Oakland, Genesee, Lapeer, and Macomb counties. Some calls are a full re-side after storm damage or years of weather; others are a single dented panel or a finish-carpentry job like new porch soffit. We take on both with the same crew and the same standard of work.

## Repairs That Start Below the Surface

Damaged siding is often a symptom, not the whole problem. Rusted or loose panels can point to moisture getting behind the wall, and loose siding sometimes means the girts underneath are failing. When we come out for a siding repair, we check the wall system, posts, girts, and fasteners, not just the panel you called about, so the fix addresses the cause.

## Finish Carpentry and Remodeling

Beyond siding panels, we handle trim, soffit, fascia, and finish-carpentry work, including projects like rebuilding a porch ceiling after a roof replacement. If a job touches the outside of a building or the finish details around it, it is very likely something we can take on.
