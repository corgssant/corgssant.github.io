## Keep Your Building Looking and Performing Its Best

Siding protects your pole barn from wind, rain, snow, and sun. When it gets damaged or deteriorates, the structure underneath is at risk. CNO Pole Barns offers comprehensive siding repair and replacement services for pole barns, garages, horse barns, and agricultural buildings throughout Oakland County. Whether you have a few dented panels from a storm or an entire wall that needs replacing, we have the materials and experience to get it done.

## More Than Cosmetic Repairs

Siding damage is often a sign of deeper issues. Rusted panels may indicate moisture problems. Loose siding can mean the underlying girts are failing. When CNO Pole Barns comes out for a siding repair, we inspect the full wall system, including posts, girts, insulation, and fasteners, to make sure the fix addresses the root cause, not just the symptom. Our repair work is built to the same standard as our new construction.
