About CNO

A Family Legacy of Quality Construction

Founded in 1970, CNO Pole Barns has been a trusted name in Michigan construction for over five decades. What started as a small family operation in Clarkston has grown into one of Oakland County's most respected pole barn builders.

Led by Zosim Serban, our team brings a hands-on approach to every project. We believe in honest work, fair pricing, and building structures that stand the test of time.
