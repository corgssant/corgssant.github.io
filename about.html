Est. 1970Clarkston, Michigan

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

CNO Pole Barns was founded in 1970 in Clarkston, Michigan, with a simple mission: build high-quality structures at fair prices. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of Southeast Michigan's most trusted construction companies.

For over 55 years, we've built everything from simple storage barns to complex equestrian facilities and modern barndominiums. Every project gets the same level of dedication, whether it's a one-car garage or a 10,000 sq ft commercial building.

Today, under the leadership of owner Zosim Serban, CNO continues the tradition of quality craftsmanship, honest communication, and building relationships that last as long as our structures.

Meet the Owner

Zosim Serban

Zosim has been in the construction industry for decades, carrying forward the family tradition that started CNO Pole Barns in 1970. His hands-on approach means he's often on-site, ensuring every project meets his high standards.
