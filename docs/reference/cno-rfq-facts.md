# CNO RFQ form - verified product facts (source: "CNO Pole Barns RFQ - Google Forms.pdf", added 2026-07-07)

CNO's own quote-request form. These are usable, owner-verified facts for site copy
and for rebuilding the quote form on /contact/ later. Form URL is in the PDF footer
(docs.google.com/forms/d/1GfJWTB5QYeU0m-txTNqQZq5OjNXHOK3UxYCAC2SqVAM).

## Options CNO quotes (= facts safe to publish)

- Styles: gable, gambrel
- Roof pitch: 3/12 through 12/12; CNO's guidance: "most standard sized barns look
  and perform best with a 4/12 pitch - also the most cost-effective option"
- Trusses: standard, storage, attic, scissor, rafters
- Roof: steel with/without OSB sheathing, or shingles
- Siding: steel with/without OSB, vinyl with OSB, T1-11, engineered wood, fiber cement
- Wainscoting available (lower-wall contrasting panels)
- Lean-tos: open or enclosed, quoted by width/length
- Overhangs: gable and eave, 6" / 12" / 16" / 24"
- Overhead doors: any count/size, insulated or non-insulated, opener optional
- Windows: single-hung, sliders, double-hung; stock sizes 24x36, 36x36, 32x48,
  36x48, 36x60, 24x24, 24x48, 48x48, 48x60
- Service doors: any count
- Concrete flooring: 4" / 5" / 6" / 7" / 8"
- Timing buckets they ask for: within 3 months, 3-6 months, 6 months-1 year, flexible

## Steel color/warranty facts (from the color chart in the form)

- 29 & 26 gauge steel panels
- 29 gauge: 45-year MS Colorfast45 warranty (galvanized substrate) or 40-year
  Colorfit40 (Galvalume substrate); 26 gauge: 45-year MS Colorfast45 (Galvalume)
- Galvalume unpainted: 25-year; plain galvanized: no warranty
- Stock colors incl.: bright white, ash gray, taupe, charcoal, brown, burnished slate,
  forest green, patriot red, burgundy, ocean blue, black, native copper, white,
  light stone, mocha tan, gray, zinc gray, carlsbad canyon, buckskin, canadian brown,
  red, hawaiian blue; non-stock colors available in 26 and 24 gauge on request
