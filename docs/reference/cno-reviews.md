## Handling the negative reviews

Every negative review except a couple has a professional owner response already written -
these are worth reading for the brand voice (accountable, quality-over-price). We are NOT
putting negatives on the site, but keep in mind:

- The recurring positive themes to lean into: **communication, quality, fair pricing,
  Zosim/Ian responsiveness, crews that clean up, custom design.**
- The recurring criticism themes (to preempt with on-site content): **timeline/weather
  delays** and **change-order / added costs**. A short "How we handle timelines & pricing"
  or FAQ section would directly answer the most common complaints.
